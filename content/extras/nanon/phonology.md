## Diphthongs

* ae - bite
* ei - bait
* ou - own
* ao - brown

All other vowel combinations either aspirate the second vowel or separate with a glottal stop.

## Accentuation

Meaning does not vary by accentuation, but usually falls on the penultimate syllable.
